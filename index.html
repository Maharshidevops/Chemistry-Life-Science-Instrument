About Our Facility

Instrumentation Facility At School Of Science

The Central Instrumentation Laboratory at GSFC University is equipped with cutting-edge analytical instruments to support advanced research and education in chemical sciences. Our facility provides researchers and students with access to sophisticated instrumentation for materials characterization, molecular analysis, and chemical synthesis.

Our mission is to foster innovation and discovery by providing access to Instrumentation Facility At School Of Science, expert training, and technical support to the university community and external partners.

Chemical Sciences Instrumentation Facility

Equipped with advanced instruments like HPLC, GC, AAS, FT-IR, AUTOLAB Potentiostat, UV-Vis, and Fluorescence Spectrometers, the department provides hands-on training and cutting-edge research opportunities. Drinking Water Analysis Laboratory (NABL Accredited)

The Drinking Water Analysis Laboratory at GSFC University received NABL accreditation on 27 November 2025. The lab ensures reliable analysis for municipal bodies, industries, and communities by covering 19 critical drinking water parameters in alignment with national standards.

Scope of Accreditation

The accreditation covers the following parameters:

  • Heavy metals: Cd, Pb, Ni, Cr, Cu, Fe, Mn, Zn
  • Physico-chemical: pH @ 27°C, Colour, Odour, Turbidity
  • Water quality indices: Total Alkalinity as CaCO3, Total Hardness as CaCO3, Electrical Conductivity @ 25°C
  • Ionic composition: Total Dissolved Solids, Chlorides (as Cl), Calcium (as Ca), Magnesium (as Mg)

Pricing (Cost of Analysis)

  • Composite Drinking Water Analysis: ₹650 + 18% GST (per sample)
